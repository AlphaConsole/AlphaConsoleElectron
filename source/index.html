Just set it up once and you're good to go, unless you want to make changes! IKR how neat is that! Please only escelate an issue to a Dev or Admin if it is critical and can't be resolved by a member of support.

Most of all, Have fun and play nice! Try to give us as much feedback as possible, both good and bad!
