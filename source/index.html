Welcome to the AlphaConsole Beta! Congratulations, you are one of the few people to help us test our new UI and app framework.


First we need to go over a few basic points.

  1. The App: Now this is the most important part! You no longer need AlphaConsole on for your item changes to take effect.... magic ...... Just set it up once and you're good to go, unless you want to make changes! IKR how neat is that! If you want to disable AC without having to uninstall then just head over to the settings tab and UnCheck "Enable AlphaConsole", hit save and BOOM no more ac.

  2. Bugs: We know not everything will be perfect thats why its beta ;)! But if you have crashes, issues, bugs, non loaded textures etc then please report them to the #beta-help channel in the Discord. If you have any other issues after that then contact support. Please only escalate an issue to a Dev or Admin if it is critical and can't be resolved by a member of support.

Most of all, Have fun and play nice! Try to give us as much feedback as possible, both good and bad!
